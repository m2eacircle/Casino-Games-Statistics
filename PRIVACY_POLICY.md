# Privacy Policy for Blackjack Statistics

**Last Updated: January 17, 2025**

## Introduction

m2ea Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Blackjack Statistics mobile application and website (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service.

## Our Commitment to Privacy

Blackjack Statistics is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device using browser local storage:

1. **Game Data**:
   - Player names (if you choose to set custom names)
   - Current coin balance for each player
   - Lock status if a player runs out of coins
   - Terms of Use acceptance status

2. **No Personal Information**: We do not collect or store:
   - Email addresses
   - Phone numbers
   - Payment information
   - Location data
   - Device identifiers
   - Any personally identifiable information

### Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to our servers
- Is not shared with third parties
- Can be deleted by clearing your browser cache or app data

## How We Use Information

The locally stored information is used solely to:
- Remember your game progress
- Track player coin balances
- Implement the 24-hour lockout feature
- Remember that you accepted the Terms of Use

## Data Sharing

**We do not share any data with third parties** because we do not collect any data from you.

We do not:
- Sell your information
- Share information with advertisers
- Provide information to analytics services
- Transmit data to external servers

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 12 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 12. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this app, you can clear the app data to remove all locally stored information.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You control the data
- Data security depends on your device's security
- We recommend:
  - Using device lock/password protection
  - Keeping your device's operating system updated
  - Being cautious about device access

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: All data is accessible through the app interface
- **Deletion**: Clear browser cache or app data to delete all information
- **Portability**: Data is stored in standard browser local storage format

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Educational Purpose

This app is designed for educational and entertainment purposes only. It is not intended for real-money gambling. No financial information is collected or processed.
